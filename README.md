The demand for delivery drivers has greatly ex
panded as a result of the rapid expansion of e-commerce
and online food delivery platforms. However, this line of work
comes with its own set of difficulties, including rushed timelines,
erratic traffic, and variable delivery routes. The purpose of
this study is to boost operational efficiency, driver approval,
and customer service. To that end, we propose a thorough
method to modeling, simulating, and optimizing the life of a
delivery driver. The study’s findings give delivery organizations
important information about how to streamline their processes
and enhance the overall satisfaction of both clients and drivers.
This study advances the logistics and delivery sector and lays the
groundwork for further research in the area by utilizing cutting
edge modeling methodologies and optimization strategies. In the
end, we want our labor to have a good effect.
